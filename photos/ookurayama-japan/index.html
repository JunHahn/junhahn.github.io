Ookurayama, Japan

Ookurayama stn. is a small subway station, where you can also have a small Japanese local community with many small shops, restaurants, markets, etc. Ookurayama has quite a lot of buildings inspired by Greek architecture styles by some historical reason. If you want to explore small town, this place would be one of the best you can have a visit in Yokohama. Photo Taken by Leica M-A, 50mm Summicron with Kodak Tri-X 400
